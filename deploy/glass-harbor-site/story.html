Act I - The Return

Lena retrieves a luxury yacht's black box after a storm and finds traces of objects that should have been logged as destroyed years earlier.

Act II-A - The Model District

Ellis helps decode the marina's design history, revealing that the redevelopment plan doubled as a map of suppressed claims and missing residents.

Act II-B - The Inventory Of Loss

As Lena and Ellis dig deeper, recovered artifacts begin triggering selective memory collapses among the people meant to benefit from the district's renewal.

Act III - Low Tide Disclosure

During a blackout evacuation, the harbor gives up the final proof, forcing the city to choose between market stability and public truth.
