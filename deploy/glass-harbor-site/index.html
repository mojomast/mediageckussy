Feature Film Package

Glass Harbor

When a drowned luxury marina starts returning impossible objects to shore, a bankrupt salvage diver and an estranged architect uncover a real-estate fraud built on engineered memory loss.

neo-noir mystery drama | 118 minute feature

Principal Characters

Lena Voss

salvage diver

A commercially licensed diver who knows the harbor's dead zones better than the developers selling waterfront serenity.

Ellis Kade

architect

A disgraced designer returning home to discover his most successful project may have been built to conceal a crime.

Eva March

marina operator

A polished executive whose talent for civic reinvention depends on nobody auditing what was erased to make it possible.
