Neon Aftercare

After a catastrophic biotech launch, a night-shift cleanup crew turns crisis management into a secret second business while trying to stay ahead of regulators, influencers, and each other.

sci-fi workplace dramedy | 8 x 45 min episodes

Characters

A compliance dropout who treats every emergency as a puzzle in incentives, leverage, and plausible deniability.

Ines Rho

A crisis storyteller who can rewrite a scandal overnight, provided nobody asks whether the new version is true.
