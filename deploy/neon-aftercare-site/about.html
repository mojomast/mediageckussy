About Neon Aftercare

The series unfolds in a near-future waterfront district where biotech startups lease converted shipping terminals and every public health failure is also a branding problem.

Themes

  • cleanup labor as invisible heroism
  • monetizing disaster
  • loyalty under regulatory pressure
  • reinventing family in crisis

Tone

  • tense
  • funny
  • humane
  • procedural with neon paranoia
